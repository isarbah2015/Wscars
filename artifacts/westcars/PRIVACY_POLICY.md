# Westcars Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 3, 2026
**App:** Westcars (`gh.westcars.app`)
**Operator:** Westcars, Accra, Ghana
**Contact:** westcarsgh@gmail.com

This policy explains what information the Westcars mobile app ("the app", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By installing or using Westcars you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
- **Account information:** name, email address, and (optionally) phone number when you sign up or sign in. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.
- **Profile information:** display name, profile photo, and location label (e.g. "Accra") that you choose to add.
- **Listings you create:** car details (make, model, year, price, description, condition, location), and photos you upload from your device's photo library.
- **Messages:** the content of in-app messages you send to other users.
- **Reports:** details you submit when reporting a listing or user.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device location** (only with your permission and only while you are using the app) to show cars near you and to attach an approximate location to listings you create. Location is used in real time and is not continuously tracked in the background.
- **Basic device & diagnostic information** (operating system, app version, crash logs) to help us fix bugs and improve reliability.

### 1.3 Information we do **not** collect
- We do **not** access your camera. The app only reads images you explicitly select from your photo library.
- We do **not** access your microphone, contacts, calendar, SMS, or call logs.
- We do **not** track your location in the background.
- We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and secure your account and let you sign in.
- Show your listings to other users and let buyers contact you.
- Display cars and dealerships near your location.
- Send transactional notifications (e.g. a message reply, a reported listing update).
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our terms.
- Improve the app and fix bugs.

---

## 3. How we share your information

We share information only in these limited cases:
- **With other Westcars users:** your public profile (name, photo, region) and the listings you publish are visible to all app users by design.
- **With service providers** that help us run the app — currently:
  - **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage for photos, Cloud Functions). Firebase processes data according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **For legal reasons:** if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Westcars, our users, or the public.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

---

## 4. Data retention

- Account and profile data are retained for as long as your account is active.
- Listings remain visible until you delete them or your account is deleted.
- Messages are retained so both parties can see the conversation history.
- When you delete your account, your profile, listings, and personal data are deleted within 30 days. Anonymized aggregate data (e.g. total listing counts) may be retained for analytics.

---

## 5. Your rights and choices

You can:
- **Access or update** your profile and listings inside the app at any time.
- **Revoke location permission** in your device's system settings; the app will continue to work without location features.
- **Request account deletion** by emailing westcarsgh@gmail.com from the address tied to your account, or by using the in-app "Delete account" option in Settings. We will delete your data within 30 days.
- **Opt out of marketing notifications** in your device's notification settings.

If you are in a jurisdiction with specific data-protection laws (e.g. GDPR, Ghana's Data Protection Act 2012), you also have the right to request a copy of your data, to object to processing, and to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

---

## 6. Children's privacy

Westcars is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the minimum age of digital consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase's managed authentication, and access-control rules on our database and storage. No system is perfectly secure, but we work hard to protect your information.

---

## 8. International transfers

Your data is processed by Google Firebase, which may store and process it on servers outside Ghana. By using Westcars you consent to the international transfer of your data, subject to the safeguards described in this policy and Google's Firebase data-processing terms.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version. Material changes will be announced inside the app. Continued use of Westcars after a change means you accept the updated policy.
