### Visual Effects:

- Cards: 16px border radius, subtle shadow (0px 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.04))
- Images: Cover fit, rounded corners
- Icons: Use Feather or MaterialIcons, 14px size, #6B7280
- Spacing: 12px between cards, 16px padding inside cards
- Typography: Inter or SF Pro font family

### Header Section:

- Gradient background: #FF6B00 to #FF8C42
- User greeting text: "Hello, [Name]" or "Hello, Guest" in white
- Notification bell icon with badge count
- Profile avatar image (or placeholder)

### Search Bar:

- White background, 14px border radius
- Padding: 12px 16px
- Search icon on left

### Category Chips:

- Loading: Skeleton shimmer effect on cards
