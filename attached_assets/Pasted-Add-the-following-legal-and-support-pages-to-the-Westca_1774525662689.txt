## 3. FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions
Create `src/screens/FAQScreen.js` with collapsible accordion sections:

**Questions & Answers:**

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| **How do I create an account?** | Sign up with your Ghana phone number (+233) and create a password. Verify via SMS. |
| **How do I sell a car?** | Tap the "Sell" button, upload photos, fill in car details, and submit. Your listing will appear after review. |
| **How do I verify my identity?** | Go to Profile → Verification Center. Upload your government ID and take a selfie. Smile ID will verify you instantly. Verified users get a badge. |
| **Is it safe to buy a car on Westcars?** | Always meet in a public place, inspect the car thoroughly, test drive, and never pay before seeing the car. Use in‑app chat to communicate. |
| **How do I contact a seller?** | Use the "Message" button on the listing or call via the provided phone number. Verified sellers have badges. |
| **How do I report a suspicious listing?** | Tap the flag icon on the listing or go to the user's profile and select "Report". |
| **What is the Trust Score?** | A score based on verification, completed sales, and user ratings. Higher scores indicate more trustworthy sellers. |
| **How do I delete my account?** | Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This removes all your data from Westcars. |
| **Can I post motorcycles?** | Yes! Use the "Мото" subcategory filter to browse or list motorcycles. |
| **How do I enable dark mode?** | Go to Profile → Settings → Theme and select Dark or Light mode. |
| **What payment methods can I use?** | Payments are between buyer and seller. Common methods: Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfer, or cash on delivery. |
| **What if I receive a suspicious phone number in chat?** | Westcars will show a warning if a number differs from the listing. Do not transact outside the app. |
| **How do I contact Westcars support?** | Email us at **westcarsgh@gmail.com** or use the Help & Support section in your profile. |
